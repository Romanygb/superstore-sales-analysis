# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis of e-commerce sales data to identify key growth drivers and develop actionable business recommendations.

## 🎯 Project Overview

- **Dataset:** Superstore Sales Dataset (9,800 records, 2015-2018)
- **Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SQL, PostgreSQL
- **Goal:** Analyze sales patterns and provide data-driven recommendations

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Total Revenue:** $2.26M over 4 years
- **Growth:** 50% increase from 2015 to 2018
- **Top Category:** Technology (36% of revenue)
- **Top Region:** California ($446K, 20% of revenue)
- **Customer Base:** Consumer segment drives majority of sales

## 📈 Visualizations

The analysis includes:
- Sales distribution histogram
- Revenue by product category
- Year-over-year sales trends
- Top 10 states by revenue
- Customer segment breakdown
- Seasonality heatmap

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Product Strategy:** Expand Technology product line
2. **Marketing:** Strengthen presence in CA, NY, and TX
3. **Customer Retention:** Develop loyalty program for Consumer segment
4. **Planning:** Leverage seasonality insights for promotional campaigns
